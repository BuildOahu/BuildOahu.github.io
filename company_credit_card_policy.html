  • Guidelines for the Use of Company Credit Cards

If you are authorized to spend money on behalf of the Company then you have a big responsibility and we didn’t think it was fair to make you decide when and where it was appropriate to use the card. So here are is a list of guidelines for appropriate use of company credit cards:

  1. You can make one purchase of gas for your work vehicle each week. Make sure this is the vehicle you are using to transport materials, or yourself, between different job sites during work hours. Also, take note that this is only one purchase, NOT one tank. If you buy a half tank of gas on Monday and another half tank on Wednesday, then that’s two purchases which is a violation. So, when you buy gas make it count for the week.

  2. Only use the credit card to purchase materials that will be consumed during the course of the job you are working on. If you wish to purchase tools, or types of goods that can be reused on multiple jobs then you need to get prior approval from James.

  3. You are responsible for returning leftover / unused materials and equipment that you purchased. Surplus goods leftover at the end of a project need to be returned, or taken to the shop. Don’t take anything you’ve purchased using the company credit card home without prior approval from James.

  4. Absolutely no personal purchases using the company card. If you need to make a purchase for your family, but you don’t have your debit card or cash. Do not use the company card. That’s not what it’s for.

  5. You are responsible for submitting receipts using the Receipt Bank app. When you send receipts, make sure you include BOTH the job / customer name and the category.

All employees with company cards are asked to review this policy. The policy has ZERO to do with lack of trust. It was written for quite the opposite reason - we want to set you up for success - one of the ways we can do that is giving you clarity and directions for proper use, so you don’t have to try and figure that out on your own.
